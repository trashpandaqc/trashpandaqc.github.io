"Wickedly challenging and invigoratingly playful in a way we’ve all come to love and expect from Conditional, Trash Panda QC’s ‘Jumps 19’ works a sort of straightjacketed funk akin to Team Doyobi and SDEM but with a deep fried electronic sauciness of his own brewing . . ." - Boomkat, "Jumps 19"
Trash Panda QC (the letters don't stand for anything) is future dance music, finding euphoria in cognitive dissonance, unfamiliarity, and a reasonable helping of sub bass. Inspired by mutant and forgotten collisions of techno, noise, and 90s rave, his music's straightforward positivity contrasts with much of the experimental scene; this is not music about the darkness of our world, it's music about creating a new one. Mind-bending sounds and evolving sequences dripping over the edges between perceived reality and artificiality give audiences a concrete experience of the unknown, recognizing that experience can be a powerful spark to change.

Sometimes performing in total darkness and sometimes with live video, recent AV shows have treated imagery in a similar way, processing found footage into an organic mulch, suggesting new life feeding on cultural debris. With the music situated within, there is a sense that the familiar can evolve, and feelings pushed out by routine can slip in through the cracks.

Trash Panda QC has released on Edvacm Editions, Co-Dependent, Conditional, and Datafruits, in addition to an extensive self-released discography. As enn{kdog, a noise duo with Shimmering Ice Puzzle, he has released on Immigrant Breast Nest and Visual Disturbances. Past releases and remixes under real name Peter Seligman have also appeared on Moss Archive, Conditional, Tsuku Boshi, and Jacktone.

Recent activities have included:
  • the Casual Internet Party - a post-pandemic continuation of a monthly bar night as a series of long-form AV broadcasts on Datafruits.fm, with guests and visualists from around the world
  • a subsequent biweekly DJ series
  • co-curating a remix compilation of that viral Guns N' Roses sample
  • a VR sound installation and soundtrack premiering on Currents.fm's Common Festival
  • Is Under Location Surfaces, an album simultaneously produced and premiered on a Resonance Extra program of the same name.
Friends and frequent sharers of bills in no particular order: Conditional Records. Abstract Conditions. Datafruits.fm. Zona Music(a). Cosmic Sound. Moss Archive. Mysterious House. Yasha. XGLARE. Engine. GS Sultan. Eve Essex. Tripletrain. DIY Church/DJ Schlucht.
