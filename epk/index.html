Trash Panda QC (the letters don't stand for anything) is a dance music producer/performer and video artist, inspired by mutant collisions of techno, 90s rave, noise, and newer polyrhythmic styles like singeli and electro acholi. His music is about finding euphoria in unfamiliarity, mixing recognizable grooves with unpredictable generative evolutions and sounds that sit on the border between reality and synthesis. Contrasting with much of experimental club music, it is less about darkness or cathartic resolutions, and more about constructing a new, ever-changing reality.

Following a long history of live performance in darkened DIY basements and underground clubs, recent Trash Panda QC live sets, evolving from quarantine livestreams and CGI experiments, have included video projections with a similar sensibility - found footage synthesized into an organic mulch, suggesting new life feeding on cultural debris. There is a sense that the familiar can evolve, and forgotten feelings pushed out by routine can slip in through the cracks. The goal is to give audiences a concrete, positive experience of the unknown, recognizing that experience can be a powerful spark for personal evolution.

Trash Panda QC has released on Conditional, Co-Dependent, and Datafruits, in addition to an extensive self-released discography. Past releases and remixes under real name Peter Seligman have appeared on Moss Archive, Tsuku Boshi, and Jacktone.

Other recent activities have involved a mix of media:
  • music video direction/animation for noise artist Mezzanine Swimmers and acid house producer ksd6700.
  • a VR sound installation and soundtrack premiering on Currents.fm's Common Festival
  • Is Under Location Surfaces, an album simultaneously produced and premiered on the Resonance Extra program of the same name.
  • the Casual Internet Party - a post-pandemic continuation of a monthly bar night as a series of long-form AV broadcasts on Datafruits.fm.
