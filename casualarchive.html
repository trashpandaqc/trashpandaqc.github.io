The "Casual" series started as a monthly-ish DJ night at Flowers For All Occasions (RIP) in 2019, and continued on the internet via Twitch and then Datafruits.fm as a series of extended broadcasts at the beginning of covid quarantine, allowing me to book a mix of artists far beyond New York. I left it up to each one whether they wanted their sets archived or not, so this page lists the full lineups and links to any sets available.
