The "Casual" series started as a monthly-ish DJ night at Flowers For All Occasions in Brooklyn, and has continued on the internet via Twitch and then Datafruits.fm since the beginning of coronavirus quarantine. From August 2020-February 2021 it became a biweekly radio show with solo DJ sets. This page archives all of the guests and guest sets from the series - for the radio show, just check this playlist or Mixcloud, and for the merch table click here.
